About Me

Currently a Senior Product Designer at Squarespace, I specialize in clarifying complex ecosystems, creating digital experiences in physical spaces, and prototyping ideas in code.

On the Trial Experience team at Squarespace I create onboarding, educational features, and cross-team collaborations that enable a coherent user experience across the full product and drive business growth. Additionally, I build tools and processes that empower collaboration between designers and engineers.

Previously I worked in Experience Design at R/GA, where I built products and tech-enabled consumer experiences for the world’s biggest brands inlcuding Samsung, Mercedes, Equinox, and Verizon.
